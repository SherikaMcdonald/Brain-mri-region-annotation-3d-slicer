Brain MRI Region Annotation and 3D Visualization Workflow Notes

Dataset:
MRBrainTumor2 sample dataset from 3D Slicer.

Objective:
The objective of this project was to annotate a visible region of interest from a brain MRI scan and generate a final 3D model suitable for portfolio presentation.

Workflow Summary:
1. Loaded the MRBrainTumor2 sample dataset in 3D Slicer.
2. Reviewed the original MRI scan in axial, sagittal, coronal, and 3D views.
3. Created a region annotation based on a visible area of interest.
4. Reviewed the annotation across multiple anatomical planes.
5. Removed small disconnected regions from the segmentation.
6. Applied smoothing to improve the final segmentation surface.
7. Generated a 3D model from the cleaned segmentation.
8. Exported the final model in STL, OBJ, and PLY formats.
9. Saved screenshots to document each major stage of the workflow.
